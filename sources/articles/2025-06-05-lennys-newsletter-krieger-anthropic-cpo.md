## Key stats

- **90-95% of Claude's code is AI-written**
- Krieger prototyped something in **25 min that would have taken 6 hours** manually
- Most companies will reach 90%+ AI code generation **"within a year"** (i.e. by mid-2026)
- Artifact (the shut-down product) had **"10 units of input for 1 unit of output"** energy efficiency
- PM teams embedded with AI researchers drive **10x more impact** than teams building on public APIs

## Direct quotes

> "Embrace who you are and what you could be rather than who others are." — Krieger on Anthropic vs OpenAI strategy

> "Work at the edge of model capabilities and be willing to break things."

> "MCP is the fastest-growing standard in tech history."

> "You can ask Claude, but I know I'm right." — Krieger quoting his daughter, on AI dependency

## Product development insights

- **New bottleneck**: engineering capacity → decision-making + merge queue management
- **Claude Opus 4** crossed a threshold to function as a genuine strategy partner, offering novel angles previously unavailable
- **Artifact shutdown**: mobile web infra limits, news distribution, remote-work coordination — strategic reallocation, not failure framing

## MCP signals

- Microsoft integrating MCP into Windows
- Vision: *"everything becomes an MCP endpoint"*
- Positioned as potentially transformative for software architecture

## Skills for the AI era (Krieger's list)

- Curiosity + scientific thinking
- Independent thought verification
- Avoiding complete outsourcing of cognition to AI
