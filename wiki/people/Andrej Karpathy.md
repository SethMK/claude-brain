# Andrej Karpathy

## Role

Former Tesla AI director, OpenAI co-founder, prolific LLM teacher (YouTube channel ~50k subscribers as of 2026). Increasingly focused on **how individuals work with LLMs** rather than how to train them.

## Why I track him

- He proposed [[Karpathy LLM Wiki|the LLM Wiki pattern]] this entire project is built on
- His takes on agent coding (e.g. moved from 20% → 80% agents in 2 months) shape the field
- Teaching-first approach — when he posts something, it's deliberately structured for learning

## Key claims / patterns

- **LLM Wiki vs RAG** — knowledge should compound, not re-derive per query
- **80% agent coding** (Jan 2026 tweet) — personal shift in working pattern
- **Markdown over embeddings** — future-LLM-proof, human-readable, gitable
- **Coined "vibe coding"** — per [[Wanderloots]] in [[2026-04-25-wanderloots-why-llm-wiki]]; the term has since become field shorthand for prompt-driven coding without spec.
